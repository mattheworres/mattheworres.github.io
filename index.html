I am a full stack web developer who loves to make truly great software.

My Skills

ASP.NET & PHP

Servers need to do powerful things, and I can make them do those things on a bunch of different platforms. .NET and PHP cover the vast majority of what's in use by real companies, and I know how to do both of them the right way.

Frontend Frameworks

AngularJS, React, Bootstrap, Foundation, and more. Web applications are no longer single page affairs, and need powerful frameworks to help do a lot of that work. Why re-invent wheels when you're inventing the next Tesla? Frameworks help make that a reality.

Communication

Ideas are only created by people, and if people cannot connect and communicate them between one another, they are no different than wind. Clients, team mates, family, you name it: high bandwidth personal communication is what I'm all about.

Collaboration

I'm confident in what I do well, but I am smart enough to know when I won't do so well. Which is why I rely on the people around me to make the software I create even better. John C. Maxwell said, "Team work makes the dream work" and I have seen it for myself to be the truth.
